2. Information We Collect

  • Login information: Username and password (stored safely and not shared).

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • No Aadhaar / PAN / ID numbers
  • No photos, contacts, SMS, call logs, or location
  • No real patient medical records
  • No financial information

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage login and membership access
  • To generate repertory analysis
  • To improve performance and fix issues
  • To provide customer support

5. Payments

All membership payments are processed exclusively through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any card, UPI, or banking information.

6. Data Security

We implement strong security measures including local encryption, obfuscated code, and secure access control. No user data is transmitted to external servers unless explicitly required.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Third-Party Services

We only use trusted Google Play services for crash reporting, analytics, and payments.
